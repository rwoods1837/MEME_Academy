Our Programs

Meme Culture- This class teaches about the culture of memes, this is the way millennials connect with one another over the internet, with the positive side effect of happiness.

Meme History- This class teaches about the long history of memes and how they started out, The word meme was coined by Richard Dawkins in his 1976 book The Selfish Gene, as an attempt to explain the way cultural information spreads; Internet memes are a subset of this general meme concept specific to the culture and environment of the Internet.

Meme Language- Often times, languages within meme culture is written in english, and point out everyday, (or not), events. With an exception to the ancient "CHEEZBURGR" and recent "BONE APPLE TEA" memes.

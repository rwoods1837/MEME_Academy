About Our School

The mission of MEME Academy is to provide each student with the opportunity to become lifelong Meme Creators by nurturing students' individuality, guiding students' learning, and challenging students to think critically through the shared involvement of teachers, administrators, parents, and community members to help them in their journey to Meme Godhood. Our purpose is to develop a student-centered learning community in which all students achieve their maximum ability through a rigorous curriculum, clearly defined standards of performance, and high expectations.
